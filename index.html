Summit Connect

Learn | It's important to get into a connect group because it helps you to spiritually grow through understanding God's word together. You can face life challenges with people who have or are facing those same challenges as you.

Serve | By serving others, it not only impacts you & your group but the people your serving. It helps others see that there are people who still care for others & are not selfish as most people.

Worship | Worshiping is a BIG concept & being in a connect group is an act of worship.
